Our working principles

Six principles.
One promise: no mail left behind.

Most inbox tools help you read faster. XMAIL is built so nothing ever falls through — from the mail that matters to the one you never want to see again.

01

Nothing gets dropped

Every email is tracked from arrival to resolution — finished, or given a reminder. There's no silent "read and forgotten" state. Ever.

02

One inbox, any channel

Dashboard, Telegram, WhatsApp — three windows into the same mail. Mark it done in one, it's done everywhere. Instantly in sync.

03

AI triages, you decide

AI reads, summarizes, prioritizes and drafts — but never sends or deletes on its own. Every reply is human-approved. Speed, with control.

04

Flag, never silently block

Scam risk — impersonation, dangerous attachments, scam phrasing — is surfaced with the exact reason. Never auto-hidden on suspicion alone.

05

Blacklist the mail, not the person

Block a specific address forever — that exact sender, not the whole domain or contact behind it. One bad actor never burns a real relationship.

06

Silence gets followed up

Replied and heard nothing? XMAIL nudges you to chase it. Reminders return exactly when you set them. Even the quiet threads get a second look.
